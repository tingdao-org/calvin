"Now Caiaphas was he, which gave counsel to the Jews, that it was expedient that one man should die for the people."
这该亚法，就是从前向犹太人发议论说，一个人替百姓死是有益的那位。

14.Who had given counsel to the Jews. The Evangelist repeats the opinion of Caiaphas, which formerly came under our notice; (136) for God employed the foul mouth of a wicked and treacherous high priest to utter a prediction, (Joh_11:50,) just as he guided the tongue of the prophet Baham, contrary to his wish, so that he was constrained to bless the people, though he desired to curse them, to gain favor with king Balak, (Num_23:7.)

(136) Vol. 1: page 453.
