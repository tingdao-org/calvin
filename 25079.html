"And he taught in their synagogues, being glorified of all."
他在各会堂里教训人，众人都称赞他。

Luk_4:15.He was glorified by all. This is stated by Luke for the express purpose of informing us, that, from the very commencement, a divine power shone in Christ, and compelled even those, who cherished a malignant spirit of contradiction, to join in admiring him.
