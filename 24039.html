30.And cast the unprofitable servant into outer darkness. We have also explained, (695) under Mat_8:12, that outer darkness is contrasted with the light which is within the house; (696) for, as banquets were anciently held, for the most part, at night, and were illuminated by numerous torches and lamps, of those who are banished from the kingdom of God, Christ says, that they are cast without into darkness

(695)Harmony, vol. 1. p. 384.

(696) “De la lumiere et clarte qui est en la maison;” — “with the light and brightness that is within the house.”
