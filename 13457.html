The womb shall forget him; the worm shall feed sweetly on him; he shall be no more remembered; and wickedness shall be broken as a tree.
怀他的母（原文作胎）要忘记他。虫子要吃他，觉得甘甜。他不再被人记念。不义的人必如树折断。
