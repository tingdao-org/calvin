But (which becometh women professing godliness) with good works.
只要有善行。这才与自称是敬神的女人相宜。

10Which becometh women; for undoubtedly the dress of a virtuous and godly woman must differ from that of a strumpet. What he has laid down are marks of distinction; and if piety must be testified by works, this profession ought also to be visible in chaste and becoming dress.
