"And he said unto her, Daughter, thy faith hath made thee whole; go in peace, and be whole of thy plague."
耶稣对她说，女儿，你的信救了你，平平安安地回去吧。你的灾病痊愈了。

Mar_5:34.Go in peace, and be delivered from thy scourge. From this exhortation we infer that the benefit which she had obtained was fully ratified, when she heard from the lips of Christ what she had already learned from experience: for we do not truly, or with a safe conscience, enjoy God’s benefits in any other way than by possessing them as contained in the treasury of his promises.
